FAQ

Questions?

What documents does FuneralOS track per case? +
Death Certificate, Burial Permit, Cremation Authorization, Contract and Invoice — each with a Missing / Pending / Complete status tracked in real time against the case file.
How does the First Call workflow work? +
When a call comes in you log the caller, relationship, decedent details, place of death and priority. A case file opens immediately with all documents set to Missing and the timeline started. No paper, no sticky notes.
Does it track staff certifications and license expiry? +
Yes. Each staff member has a certification expiry date. FuneralOS alerts you automatically when a license is expiring within 30 days — before it becomes a compliance issue.
Can I switch plans at any time? +
Yes. Upgrade to Pro or downgrade to Free whenever you like. Your case history and data are always kept safe.
Is there a mobile app? +
FuneralOS is a Progressive Web App (PWA) — install it directly from your browser on iPhone or Android. No App Store, no update approvals.
Is my data secure? +
Data is stored encrypted on Supabase cloud servers. It's also cached locally on your device so you can work offline. We never share data with third parties.
What happens when I reach 5 cases on the free plan? +
You'll be prompted to upgrade to Pro. Your existing cases are never deleted or locked — they stay fully accessible.
